Payments on site
Token system only inside the festival — top up by card or cash at stations across the grounds. Bring €50–100 cash per person as backup. Tokens are refundable at exit.
Getting there
Nearest hub: Tilburg Central Station (~20 min shuttle). Amsterdam Sloterdijk shuttles also run. Check the official Awakenings site for times closer to the date.
Meetup plan
Cell signal will be terrible with 80,000 people on one tower. Agree on a physical meetup spot inside the festival before going in — pick a stage name + area, not just "near the entrance".

Festival info
🎟
Tickets & entry
▼
📋
Personalise your ticket
Mandatory. Tickets are sent to the main buyer on Thu 8 Jul. Check your inbox for "Bleep" — also check spam.
📱
At the gate
Turn screen brightness up. Download tickets in advance. No printing needed. No re-entry once you leave the festival.
🪪
ID required
18+ event. Passport or ID card mandatory at entry — no exceptions. Bring the physical document.
🚪
Entrances
Fri: Entrance South only (Westrik 2, Hilvarenbeek)
Sat + Sun: Entrance North or South
Check which entrance is printed on your ticket.
🚌
Getting there
▼
🚆
Recommended route
Train to Tilburg Central Station, then official shuttle bus (~20 min). Easiest option for most visitors.
🚌
Shuttle from Tilburg — prices
Grand Camping / Festival South: €9.25 pre-sale / €12.50 on the day
Comfort Camping: €14.95 pre-sale / €17.50 on the day
Buy in advance — saves money and guarantees a seat.
⏰
Shuttle times from Tilburg
Fri: 11:00 – 02:30 (Entrance South)
Sat + Sun: 12:00 – 02:30 (Entrance North)
Mon: 08:00 – 12:30 (back to Tilburg)
✈️
Nearest airports
Eindhoven (EIN) — 40 km, ~30 min drive. Closest.
Amsterdam Schiphol (AMS) — 120 km, ~1.5h
Brussels (BRU) — 130 km, ~1.5h
⚠️
Leaving — allow extra time
80,000 people leave at once. Shuttles have queues. If you have a train to catch, leave the festival before the last set ends.
💶
Tokens & payment
▼
🪙
How it works
Everything inside the festival uses tokens. Top up by cash or card at token stations across the grounds. Unused tokens are refundable at exit.
♻️
Recycle token system
You get 1 free recycle token at the entrance. Every drink order: hand in your recycle token or an empty cup/can. Lose it and you pay an extra token. Return your empties at the bar or recycle points to get a token back.
💧
Free water
Free water taps at all toilet facilities and several water points near the stages. Bring your own bottle and refill for free all day.
🥗
Food
100% vegetarian menu at all stalls. Vegan and gluten-free options available. No outside food allowed inside the festival grounds (camping only).
🏕
Camping rules
▼
🍺
Drinks you can bring to camp
Max 4.5L per person, of which max 1L can be alcohol. No glass. No pre-mixed drinks above 15% ABV. All must be in original sealed packaging. No drinks from camp into the festival.
🍜
Food at camp
You can bring your own food for personal consumption. No open fires, BBQs, or camping stoves (gas or otherwise). No candles.
🔊
Speakers at camp
Small battery speakers allowed, max 80 dB, max 30 cm size. Respect your neighbours.
🏊
Lake swimming
Allowed only at designated areas and during designated hours. Not anywhere, not anytime.
🧹
Leave no trace
Strict policy across the entire campsite. Pack out everything. Camping closes Mon 13 Jul at 10:00 — leave before then.
🔒
Lockers
▼
📦
Sizes
Standard: 22 × 25 × 50 cm (1–2 people)
Large: 33 × 25 × 50 cm (2–3 people)
🛒
Buy in advance
Pre-sale deadline: Tue 7 Jul, 09:00. Locker number + PIN sent on Wed 8 Jul. Campsite lockers are pre-sale only — not available on location.
📌
PIN code
No deposit. Keep your PIN safe — losing it means anyone can access your locker. Weekend locker keeps the same code all 3 days.
🚫
What's not allowed
▼
💊
Zero tolerance on drugs
All drugs forbidden — hard and soft. Pills, powders, liquids, mushrooms, weed/hash, N2O (laughing gas). Caught = removed from festival + possible police handover. This is strictly enforced, not just written policy.
📷
Cameras
Phone cameras and compact cameras: OK
Professional cameras with detachable/interchangeable lenses: Forbidden
Drones: Forbidden
🚬
Smoking & vaping
Not sold on site. Forbidden inside venues and tents (Dutch law). Smoke only outside. Weekend allowance: max 8 packs per person. No cigarettes sold anywhere on site — bring your own.
⛺
Party tents
Not allowed Actively enforced at check-in. Will be confiscated. Only approved tents from the rental store or your own sleeping tent.
🚫
Also forbidden
Glass packaging · Fireworks · Weapons · Pets · Climbing trees or scaffolding · Crowd surfing · Open fires · Gas stoves · Fridges · Beer kegs · Candles
🆘
Safety & first aid
▼
🏥
First aid + social safety
First aid station and Social Safety Hub are next to each other near the entrance. Social Safety Team walks the grounds — look for the jackets. You can also start a WhatsApp conversation with on-site staff by scanning QR codes at the entrance or toilets.
🤫
Quiet space
Low-stimulus area with seats and noise-cancelling headphones near the first aid — useful if you get overwhelmed.
👜
Pickpockets
Busy crowd = pickpocket risk. Keep your phone in a front pocket or zipped bag. Leave expensive jewellery at home.
🔍
Lost & found
iLost tent near the first aid at the festival. After the festival, check ilost.nl
💊
Prescription medication
Allowed with an official doctor's note. First aid team checks both document and medication at entrance. Pre-contact: accessibility@awakenings.nl
🔋
Charging & lockers on site
▼
⚡
Charging stations
Charging stations at the camp store (reception) and at the locker desk on the festival site. You can also rent powerbanks on site — €30 deposit (€5/hour, refund on return).
📶
WiFi
Free WiFi zones at selected locations across the site. Don't rely on it — signal is congested with 80k people.
🛍
Click & collect shop
▼
🖥
Pre-order before you arrive
Awakenings has an online Click & Collect shop — order drinks, food, camping gear, merch, and experiences (massages, wellness) in advance. Pick up at the designated Click & Collect point on site. Skips queues and guarantees stock. Worth checking before you go.
🏪
Camp store
On-site camp store sells food, drinks, camping necessities, and hygiene products. Camping gear can also be rented (chairs, tables) at the Camping Recycle & Repair store.
